About Us

Pinnamaya Cycles

Pinnamaya Cycles is owned by Surjan Trading Private Ltd(STPL), Nagpur.
Surjan Trading Private Ltd(STPL) was established in the year 2014 under the umbrella of "Bhagyashree Group" as a trading & distribution arm for the varied products ranging from Bicycles, tyre and tubes.
Over the years it has built a niche market for its products by ensuring varied product range being offered under one roof to retailers and dealers with ready stock . The company is located in heartland of Central India in the city of Nagpur and having marketing and distribution network throughout the country with branch offices in Mumbai and Raipur.
Shri Mohanlalji Surjan present head of the parent group is founder and architect of the company. Now under the able leadership and guidance of Mr. Pravin Surjan the company has achieved more than 20% growth rate on year to year basis and is continuing the legacy seed sown by his father where he started the business from a small shop which has now carved itself as STPL with multi city location across India. Buoyed by the same STPL has now launched its own brand of bicycles developed, tested & designed in Germany under the brand name "PINAMAYA" {" BURN FATS, NOT FUEL").
"PINAMAYA" has huge range of bicycle are the young and modern cycles for today's generation. All bicycles are of modern designs with Alloy frames, Alloy rims, inner Decals, Vibrant and Florescent colours, Equipped with SHIMANO Gears and many other Features. PINAMAYA bicycles are especially designed and engineer in order to meet the requirement of upcoming cycling enthusiasts whether is it city biking, adventuring & mounting.
PINNAMAYA bicycles will be catering to all today's list that to very economical price range.
